Spread of the National Anthem

To the Editor of the Morning Post

Sir⁠—I beg to say that I have assumed the duties of Honorary Secretary to the National Anthem Extension Fund, and that all communications relating thereto should be forwarded to me at Purcell House, Dean’s-yard, Westminster, until such time as a regular office shall have been taken by the Committee.⁠—Yours, etc.,

Algernon Blackwood

5, Langham-chambers, Portland-place, August 1.
